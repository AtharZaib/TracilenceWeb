About Team & Application

Providing Students an Easy Plaform to increase their Efficiency & Hardworking

Tracilence is basically a project which is done by a group of 3 Students who found this in vain that the transport service needs any automatic & trackable system that helps them to manage their Time and Efficiency.

As a student, i am little bit lazy. As i used to travel through shuttle services, i don't know the time when the shuttle service will be at my stop. Some times , the shuttle service was closed due to some reason and i was there waiting for it. i can't figure out if the shuttle service will come from this way or not, so for this purpose , We develop Tracilence.

Single Application for Every Plaform

Tracilence is the application which is not only based for Android Users but also designed and developed for iOS Users too. Also as it is suitable for University students so those who can't afford Smartphone can also access this feature through our Web Portal .

Designed in Pakistan

Tracilence is meticulously designed
by Students of UBIT, Karachi University.
